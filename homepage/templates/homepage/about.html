ABOUT US

Welcome to the official website of NCC Farook College.

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) is the youth wing of the Armed Forces of India. At Farook College, our NCC unit plays a vital role in shaping disciplined, responsible, and patriotic citizens. We provide students with opportunities for leadership, community service, physical training, and adventure activities.

Our cadets actively participate in various national-level camps, parades, and social initiatives, proudly representing the values of Unity and Discipline. With the guidance of dedicated ANOs and trainers, we aim to build strong character and inspire a spirit of selfless service among youth.

Join us, and be a part of a legacy that builds the nation with courage and commitment.
